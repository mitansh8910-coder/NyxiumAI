Privacy Policy for Nyxium AI

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Data Collection

Nyxium AI collects minimal data necessary to provide our services. This includes Discord User IDs, Guild (Server) IDs, and command configuration settings. We do not store private message content or sensitive personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Data

Data is utilized strictly to ensure bot functionality, such as maintaining user preferences, managing music playback queues, and enforcing server moderation settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Gemini 1.5 Flash to process AI queries. Please note that data sent to the AI is subject to Google's privacy policies. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third-party advertisers.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You maintain full control over your data. You may request the permanent deletion of your stored settings or history at any time by contacting us via our official support server.
